About Us

The Tethers Token Ton (USDTs) project explores the feasibility of implementing a stablecoin on the Toncoin blockchain. The goal is to create a digital asset pegged to 1 USD for academic research and testing purposes in decentralized financial ecosystems.

Features

  • Price Stability: Pegged to 1 USD.
  • Decentralized and secure, based on Toncoin blockchain.
  • Max supply of 4,000,000,000 USDTs.
